Patty Hughes

"I am a self taught artist who has been drawing and painting for about 20 years. I have attended many workshops by outstanding instructors, and I have won awards for my work over the years. I work mostly in watercolor, acrylic, collage and mixed media and enjoy creating abstract and non-objective pieces. Since I started painting late in life, I was not sure of my purpose, but felt the strong pull to discover what I was meant to create.

What a journey it has been and continues to be!! Much of my work is intuitive, discovering where I am going in a painting as I travel through the process. Some of my work is deliberate, perhaps a mixed media piece of an incredible mountain scene or an abstract representation of a raptor in flight. My love of nature and my surroundings is evident in my plein air pieces along with my work done in the studio. I hope that my work evokes emotion when the resulting product is revealed. I love to share the gift I have been given with others!"
